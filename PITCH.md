# Pitching Sentry AI

When demoing this live for a job interview, hackathon, or to your peers, use this verbal framework.

## The Script

> "When people use AI today, they trade their privacy for convenience by uploading their data to the cloud. I built Sentry AI to solve this. It's a progressive web app that runs Large Language Models entirely in the browser's sandbox. It detects the user's hardware—like whether they have WebGPU or just standard mobile RAM—and dynamically loads the right model. It's not designed to be an encyclopedia; it's designed to be a secure, air-gapped processor for your most sensitive documents and tasks."

## Core Philosophical Points (Problem, Solution, Reality)

**1. The Problem Space**: Everyone wants AI features, but businesses and security-conscious individuals cannot afford to upload proprietary documents to remote vendor servers.

**2. The Solution**: Sentry AI brings the model *to the data*. By executing the LLM client-side as a Progressive Web App, it provides completely offline, serverless intelligence. 

**3. The Reality Check**: The reality is that local models aren't GPT-4. We turn this limitation into a feature by actively managing expectations. We emphasize *task fulfillment* (summarization, local RAG) over generalized trivia, allowing the miniature AI models to punch way above their weight class while guaranteeing absolute privacy.
